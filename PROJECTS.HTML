Brand Identity

VELOSHOPY X NESSPRESO

I WORKED ON A PROJECT FOR A COLLABORATION BETWEEN VELOSHOPY, A BICYCLE BRAND THAT USES RECYCLED ALUMINUM, AND NESSPRESO, FOR A SPECIAL EDITION MADE FROM RECYCLED COFFEE CAPSULES. I FOCUSED ON CREATING A LOGO THAT BALANCED BOTH BRANDS AND CONVEYED MOVEMENT AND DYNAMISM. THE FINAL LOGO IS COMPOSED OF A LOGOTYPE AND AN ICON REPRESENTING A BICYCLE EMERGING FROM THE "V" OF THE LOGOTYPE, WHICH FORMED THE BASIS FOR THE REST OF THE BRAND IDENTITY, INCLUDING COLOR PALETTE AND APPLICATION TO VARIOUS OBJECTS.

AINHOA MONTES

I WORKED ON A PROJECT FOR AN INTERIOR DESIGN STUDIO LED BY A DESIGNER CALLED AINHOA MONTES, WHERE I CREATED THE BRAND IDENTITY FROM THE GROUND UP. I FOCUSED ON USING SIMPLE FORMS AND A PALETTE OF BROWN TONES TO CONVEY CALM, WARMTH, AND A SENSE OF CLOSENESS. THIS VISUAL APPROACH CONNECTS NATURALLY WITH THE MINIMALIST INTERIOR DESIGN AESTHETIC AND REFLECTS A BALANCED, SERENE, AND APPROACHABLE BRAND PERSONALITY.

OCEAN BOOK

I WORKED ON AN EDITORIAL PROJECT WHERE I CREATED THE BOOK LAYOUT, COMPOSITION, VISUAL BALANCE, AND DIE-CUT ELEMENTS. THE DESIGN FOCUSES ON CREATING A COHESIVE VISUAL LANGUAGE THAT REFLECTS THE THEME OF THE OCEAN ENVIRONMENT. I ALSO DEVELOPED THE ADVERTISING CAMPAIGN, INCLUDING THE VISUAL CONCEPT, GRAPHIC PIECES, AND OVERALL COMMUNICATION STRATEGY TO PROMOTE THE BOOK SERIES. THIS PROJECT IS PART OF A TRILOGY EXPLORING JUNGLE, OCEAN, AND SAVANNAH ENVIRONMENTS, EACH WITH A UNIQUE PERSONALITY BUT A COHESIVE VISUAL LANGUAGE THAT CONNECTS ALL THREE BOOKS.

MADRID, DOOR OF ÁLCALA

I DEVELOPED A GRAPHIC PROJECT INSPIRED BY THE PUERTA DE ALCALÁ IN MADRID. THE DESIGN REINTERPRETS THIS ICONIC MONUMENT THROUGH A CLEAN AND STRUCTURED ILLUSTRATION, FOCUSING ON GEOMETRY, SYMMETRY, AND DETAIL.

I APPLIED THE VISUAL CONCEPT ACROSS DIFFERENT FORMATS, SUCH AS A POSTER AND A TOTE BAG, ENSURING THE DESIGN WORKED CONSISTENTLY IN BOTH EDITORIAL AND PRODUCT CONTEXTS. THE GREEN COLOR PALETTE GIVES THE PIECE A CONTEMPORARY AND ELEGANT FEEL, WHILE STILL RESPECTING THE HISTORICAL CHARACTER OF THE MONUMENT. THE PROJECT AIMS TO CELEBRATE MADRID'S CULTURAL HERITAGE THROUGH A MODERN GRAPHIC LANGUAGE, COMBINING ILLUSTRATION, COMPOSITION, AND APPLICATION IN A COHESIVE VISUAL SYSTEM.

DGT ILLUSTRATION

I CREATED THIS ILLUSTRATION AS PART OF A ROAD SAFETY AWARENESS PROJECT FOR THE DGT OF SPAIN. THE PIECE FOCUSES ON THE DANGERS OF DRIVING UNDER THE INFLUENCE OF ALCOHOL AND THE SERIOUS CONSEQUENCES THAT CAN RESULT FROM A SINGLE DECISION.

THE SCENE SHOWS THE AFTERMATH OF A NIGHTTIME CAR CRASH, USING RAIN, DARKNESS, AND DESATURATED COLORS TO CREATE A COLD AND DISTURBING ATMOSPHERE. ELEMENTS SUCH AS THE DAMAGED CAR, THE EMPTY ROAD, AND THE BOTTLE ON THE GROUND REINFORCE THE MESSAGE IN A DIRECT AND EMOTIONAL WAY.

THE OBJECTIVE OF THE ILLUSTRATION IS PREVENTION. IT ENCOURAGES PEOPLE TO STEP IN AND STOP SOMEONE FROM DRIVING IF THEY HAVE BEEN DRINKING, REMINDING VIEWERS THAT INTERVENING IN TIME CAN SAVE LIVES THEIR OWN AND THOSE OF OTHERS.
